What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in weather patterns and global temperatures, primarily caused by human activities such as burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial processes.

Main Causes

  • Excessive greenhouse gas emissions
  • Deforestation and land use changes
  • Overreliance on fossil fuels

Solutions

We can reduce the effects of climate change by:

  • Adopting renewable energy sources
  • Planting trees and conserving forests
  • Reducing waste and promoting recycling
  • Spreading awareness and supporting policies for sustainability
